Content Removal / DMCA Policy

Content Safety & Compliance

At ExoLust, user safety, consent, and legal compliance are top priorities.

We strictly prohibit:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Non-consensual or exploitative content
  • Revenge content or blackmail
  • Content exposing private or personal information
  • Illegal or abusive material

Any such content is removed immediately upon verification.

Copyright Notice

ExoLust operates as a content aggregation and discovery platform. We do not host video files on our own servers.

In accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA), 17 U.S.C. § 512, ExoLust qualifies as a service provider and follows DMCA Notice & Takedown procedures.

We respond promptly to valid copyright complaints.

How to Request Content Removal

To submit a valid removal request, please provide:

  • The exact URL(s) of the content
  • A clear explanation of the issue
  • Proof of ownership, consent, or authority
  • Valid contact information

Requests that lack sufficient details may not be processed.

What Can Be Reported

You may submit a complaint for:

  • Non-consensual content
  • Copyright infringement
  • Exposure of private or personal data
  • Harassment or abusive material
  • Content violating local or international laws

All reports are handled confidentially and professionally.

⚠️ Important Notice
Submitting false, misleading, or repeated spam complaints may result in access restrictions.
